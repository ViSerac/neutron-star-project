Galactic Heatmap
Top-down view of neutron star positions projected onto the Galactic plane (x/y). The Sun is located at x = -8.122 kpc. Concentration near the Sun reflects detection bias — closer objects are easier to observe. KDE mode uses Gaussian kernel density estimation with Silverman bandwidth — may take a few seconds for large datasets.
